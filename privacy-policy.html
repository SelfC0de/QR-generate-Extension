Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 14, 2024

Introduction

QR Page Generator by SelfCode ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices.

Data Collection

Our extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All QR code generation happens locally in your browser.

How It Works

When you enter a URL, the extension generates a QR code entirely within your browser. No data is sent to external servers or stored anywhere.

Data Usage

Since we don't collect any data, we:

  • Don't store any personal information
  • Don't track your browsing activity
  • Don't share any information with third parties
  • Don't use cookies or similar tracking technologies

Permissions

The extension requires no special permissions to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
